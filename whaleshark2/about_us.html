Our Story

We started 37 years ago in Singapore when Yuan Hong opened a SPA in Toa Payoh. Her dedication to massage practices brought about loyal clients in Singapore and words began to spread of this unique massage sequence that combines the best practices in China, Sweden, and Thailand.

Her Filipino husband encouraged Yuan Hong to share her curated massage sequences with the residents of the Philippines. Transferring her knowledge to Philippine talent wasn't easy but months of rigorous training paid off, and the story of Cedar Wellness SPA began.
